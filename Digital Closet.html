Digital Closet

Project Overview

Challenges

The users’ current outfit planning experience is time consuming, physically draining and messy.

Objective

Through this app users outfit planning experience will be efficient, fast and effortless.

Research - Competitive Analysis of Similar Apps

Cladwell App

Pros

- If you don't want to take photos, you can clip images from the web
- Outfit suggestions take weather into account
- Gives detailed statistics about your closet

Cons

- Can’t make tweaks or changes to outfit suggestions
- Can’t create own outfits

Pureple Outfit Planner App

Pros

- Allows user to enter many details about item
- Allows user to create their own outfits and share them
- Has social community to get outfit inspiration from

Cons

- Doesn’t show the weather
- Doesn’t give user control over closet organization

Research - User Interviews

User Interview Plan

I will interview 10 people that represent my target audience. These people will be in the age ranges of 18 and 60. Through the interviews, I will understand how they plan their outfit and organize their closets. I will learn about their process and their frustrations.

User Interview Questions

1. How do you pick out an outfit?
2. If you lost a pair of pants or top, how would you describe it?
3. What are any problems you face with your closet currently?
4. If you would transform your closet digitally, what would you want?
5. How do you organize your closet?
6. What makes it easy for you to find clothes in your closet?
7. Do you ever create pre-set outfits? If you could digital save outfits would you?
8. Do you ever get other opinions when planning an outfit - how?
9. If you could generate data about your closet, would you want to know?

User Interview Results

Research - Key Takeaways/ Insights

Key Features to Include / What Users Want

1. Weather - Because it is a key factor for users to consider when planning an outfit
2. Ability to share outfit with others
3. Let users add details about each clothing item - Because it helps them find the item
4. Give users’ control over the organization of their digital closet - Because each user has a different way of organizing and categorizing their clothes
5. Users should be able to save outfits
6. Users would like statistics or data about their closet and outfit planning if possible

User Analysis & Personas

Stacy

Stacy is 20 years old. She frequently has trouble figuring out what to wear. She usually ends up trying on all her clothes and then has to deal with hanging all of it up again. She wants a efficient way to go through her closet without making a mess. She also wants to be able to plan outfit to wear faster.

Max

Max is 38 years old. Max has a very busy schedule and usually has no time to plan out what to wear for work. He gets up very earlier in the morning and usually grabs whatever he can find. He wants a way that he can plan his outfit for the next day quickly and without physically going through his closet.
