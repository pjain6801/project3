Spotify Playlist Mixer

Built around Spotify's design principles that empowers users to personalize and diversify their music

Project Overview

Challenges

While using Spotify, I noticed that there was no feature that allows you to mix multiple playlists. Users have to switch between playlists manually. They cannot simply shuffle multiple playlists to suit their listening preferences at a given time.

Objective

1. Create a feature that allows users to mix/ shuffle playlists
2. Promote individualized music and give users more control

Research

Research Questions

How do users engage with Spotify's current playlist features?
How do users diversify and adapt playlists to their preferences?

Research Method

We used surveys as our method of research because we wanted to reach the most amount of users. Our target user audience age range (17-26) overlapped with Instagram's users, making Instagram the ideal platform to deliver our survey. Because Instagram posts and stories hold people's attention for a brief amount of time, I chose a quick survey method on Instagram to engage with target users.

Discussion

My research confirmed the user need for a feature that combines playlists. The data shows that users frequently change their playlists, which can become distracting. 90% of the participants shuffle their playlists, so designing a sophisticated shuffle feature would benefit this large group of users.

Product Vision

Interactivity

Helps users visualize how they want to build their unique Playlist Mixer.

Users can control the exact content of their playlist mixer.

Each Spotify listening experience is unique. This feature allows for "in the moment" personalization.

Screens

The main three screens are shown above. When the user is ready to select the playlists they want to add to their Playlist Mixer, they would press "select" on the top right hand corner. Then, they are prompted with slider bars. The slider bars are visual and interactive to allow users to visualize and manipulate their desired playlist balance. This feature allows users to control and mold their music to their "in the moment" preferences.

Usability Testing

After conducting usability testing with 8 Spotify users, I found two usability issues that I fixed. One issue was that the 'select' button was hard for users to locate. Therefore, I made the button larger and changed the color to increase contrast.

The second problem was that when users selected a playlist, they tried to click on the title, album, or blank space. Therefore, I made it was all clickable to create a user-friendly experience.
