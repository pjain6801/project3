Design Elements

Wishlist

Based on the findings, all the competitors have a wishlist button so customers can save items they like in a different location other than their cart. I created a heart shaped Wishlist button in the top right hand corner of each product.

New Items/ Clearance Tags

New Items/ Clearance Tags Based on the competitive analysis findings, I applied the New items and Clearance tags to my design. This will not only help users distinguish between the different types of items but also create an alternative and efficient user flow.

Quickview

In my design of the product page, I added a quick view button. This will allow customers to look at a product as a quick preview and return to their previous screen with ease. Placing the button in the middle lets the customers view it easily.

Language Dropdown

To diversify this website more, I added a change language drop down menu in a visible spot. This makes it more accessible for those who wish to change the language. It also broadens the users for this website.
