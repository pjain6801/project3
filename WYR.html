WYR

An informational tool that integrates multi-platform data to help students learn about and choose classes.

Project Overview

Based on the market and preliminary research, we found that students feel that they do not have adequate resources and information when they are choosing classes and professors. While students have platforms like the school's course catalog/ descriptions, rate my professor and reddit they want a platform where they can access all this information at once. This led to our ideation process where we designed mockups for a website that consolidated all the data from these platforms.

Project Length: 6 months
Role: UX Designer
Design Team Size: 9 people
